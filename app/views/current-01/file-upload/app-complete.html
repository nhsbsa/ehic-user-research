If you have plans to travel and do not have your card

If you don’t have your card with you and need treatment while you’re abroad, you can apply for a Provisional Replacement Certificate (PRC).

A PRC will give you the same entitlement as your [entitlement] and will cover you if you need treatment while you’re abroad.

You should only apply for a PRC at the point you need treatment. PRCs cannot be issued in advance.

To apply for a PRC while you’re abroad contact the NHS Overseas Healthcare Services. You can do this by telephone:
