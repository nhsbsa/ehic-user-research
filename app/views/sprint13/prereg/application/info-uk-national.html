Applying for an EHIC

You are eligible for a UK-issued EHIC as you've told us that you:

  • are a UK national
  • were resident in the UK before 1 January 2021
  • are not studying or do not plan to study in the EU, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway or Switzerland.
