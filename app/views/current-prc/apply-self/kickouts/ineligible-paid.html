You cannot apply for a Provisional Replacement Certificate

You cannot use this service to apply for a PRC as you've told us that you've paid more than a co-payment or patient contribution for your treatment.

You need to pay the full amount of treatment costs and then claim a refund.

Claiming a refund

Depending on the country you visit you may be expected to pay all or part of your bill upfront and then claim a refund afterwards.

You can claim back the difference between the total bill and the co-payment, but the actual co-payment is not refundable.

You should keep all invoices, receipts, and any other paperwork. You or your insurance company may need them if you apply for a refund.
