Get healthcare cover for visiting the EU (EHIC)

Use this service to apply for an EHIC (European Health Insurance Card). An EHIC gives you the right to access state-provided healthcare on temporary stays in countries in the EU/EEA and Switzerland.

You can get an EHIC if:
  • You are a UK citizen
  • You are an EU/EEA or Swiss citizen with either settled or pre-settled status or you have indefinite leave to remain in the UK
