Before you start

You'll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • the National Insurance numbers of any other people you are applying for who are 16 years old or over
Information:

Depending on your circumstances we may also ask for your NHS number, if you have one.

You only need to apply for cover if you have plans to travel in the EU, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein, or Switzerland.


If you already have a European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)

If your circumstances haven’t changed, such as you have not moved to a different country, you can continue to use your EHIC until it expires. You can apply for a new card up to 6 months before its expiry date.


My EHIC is lost or stolen

If your EHIC is lost or stolen you will need to contact the Overseas Healthcare Service to request a replacement.
