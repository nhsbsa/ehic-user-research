Healthcare cover for travelling abroad (GHIC and EHIC)

Advice about applying for healthcare cover abroad

  • Apply for healthcare cover abroad (GHIC and EHIC)

    Find out if you're eligible to apply for a new UK European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) or UK Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC)

  • Apply for the Provisional Replacement Certificate (PRC)

    If you are having an accident or injury abroad and don't have your healthcare insurance card, you can qualify for a temporary replacement card to cover for medical state-funded treatment up to 28 days.

  • Claiming a refund

    Apply for a refund if you are having an accident or injury while travelling abroad and have paid or started paying towards your treatment.
