Get healthcare cover for travelling abroad

If you or someone you know needs healthcare cover abroad, you may be entitled to help with your healthcare costs with a:

  • new UK European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)
  • UK Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC)
  • Provisional Replacement Certificate (PRC)

This website explains your options and what help is available.

  • Apply for healthcare cover abroad

    If you're planning to travel abroad, find out if you're eligible for a UK EHIC or UK GHIC to help with your healthcare costs.

  • Temporary cover for emergency treatment abroad

    If you don't have a UK EHIC or UK GHIC and need emergency treatment abroad, you can still get help with paying for your treatment with a PRC.
