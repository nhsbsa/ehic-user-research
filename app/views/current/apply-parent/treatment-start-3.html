Treatment date

You can only apply for a PRC from the time your child needs treatment. This includes treatment your child has had already.

Your child's PRC will last for 28 days starting from the first day of treatment. If the treatment lasts for longer than 28 days, you will need to reapply.

You cannot apply for a PRC in advance.
