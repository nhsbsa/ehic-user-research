If you are travelling soon

You can get a Provisional Replacement Certificate (PRC) to prove your entitlement to medically necessary healthcare if you travel to the EU, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein or Switzerland without your UK GHIC, UK EHIC or EHIC and need treatment during your visit. This includes if you travel and require treatment before you have received your card.

You should only apply for a PRC at the point you need treatment. PRCs cannot be issued in advance.

To apply for a PRC contact NHS Overseas Healthcare Services.
